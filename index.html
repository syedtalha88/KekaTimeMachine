2015 – The Beginning

It all started in a tiny room filled with big dreams and even bigger coffee mugs. Payroll nightmares were rampant, and we knew something had to change.

No fancy setups, no shiny tech—just a handful of rebels with laptops, trying to fix HR with code and sheer willpower.

And thus, Keka was born. From chaos came clarity, and from caffeine came creativity.

2016 – Early Adopters

The first 100 customers joined us on this crazy ride. They didn’t just use the platform—they believed in it.

Our celebrations weren’t extravagant, but samosas and a team high-five felt like winning a startup Oscar.

Every bit of feedback became a feature, and every bug squashed brought us closer to greatness.

2017 – Product Expansion

This year, we expanded our horizons. Performance reviews, goal tracking, and employee engagement tools rolled out like confetti.

Employees appreciated the clarity. Managers... well, they had mixed feelings—but deep down, they knew it was needed.

Keka transformed from a payroll tool to an all-in-one HR suite. It wasn’t evolution—it was a glow-up.

2018 – Gaining Momentum

We crossed 1,000 customers—and with it, our servers learned what stress truly means.

Our engineering team became nocturnal. Support tickets became love letters. And Slack emojis became our second language.

Every milestone felt like magic. Every crash became a lesson. We were learning fast, and growing even faster.

2020 – Remote Revolution

The pandemic flipped the world upside down. But Keka stood firm.

We turned remote chaos into organized calm. From virtual onboarding to WFH attendance, our tools kept teams connected and HR sane.

Behind the scenes? Zoom calls in pajamas, productivity in slippers, and a fridge that never stayed full.

2021 – Scaling Fast

With 5,000+ customers now onboard, Keka was no longer a best-kept secret—it was the new HR standard.

Our infrastructure scaled, our features matured, and our coffee intake doubled. Again.

Every success story felt personal. We weren’t just growing—we were helping others grow too.

2022 – Global Reach

Keka passports stamped across continents. Bonjour, Namaste, and Hola became daily greetings.

Global clients brought diverse needs, and we answered with flexible, intelligent solutions.

Whether in Bangalore or Berlin, we proved that great HR tech knows no borders.

2023 – Product Renaissance

This year was all about reinvention. A shiny new UI. Dashboards that even data-haters adored. Automation so smooth, it felt like magic.

Feedback drove innovation. Design met function. And spreadsheets everywhere wept quietly in the corner.

Keka became more than usable—it became lovable.

2024 – Culture First

Beyond tools and tech, we focused on what really matters—people.

Happiness scores, wellness initiatives, and culture dashboards weren’t just nice-to-haves—they became essential.

We measured engagement. Encouraged breaks. And celebrated small wins (and chai breaks) like champions.
